# SpendWise

**SpendWise** is a small Python project designed to help you mange your monthly income and expenses.

## Overview
This simple program allows you to enter your monthly income and track your bills.
It then shows you what remains after all expenses.

## Features
1. Display all entered bills with their amounts.
2. Warns you if you've spent more than 80% of your income.
3. Shows how much money is left after paying all the bills. \
